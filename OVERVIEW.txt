
Notes on Zeo++ revision (M. Haranczyk, Feb 2014)


The revised version of Zeo++ is organized around jobs.

Each job has:

Tasks (summary of what Zeo++ is going to do)

Material  [optionally a list of structures]

Output(functions to handle I/O)




 
Each Material has:

-Coordinates of atoms (basically the atom net)
-optional High Accuracy (sphere approx.) copy
-voronoi objects
-surface area/AV/PSD objects
